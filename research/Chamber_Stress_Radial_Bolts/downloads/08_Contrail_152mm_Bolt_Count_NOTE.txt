## Design takeaway

Contrail’s **6 radial fasteners per end** is a certified commercial pattern on a **~0.25″ (6.35 mm)** aluminum wall — about **2×** the wall thickness of this project’s preferred OnlineMetals **6.0″ × 0.125″** tube.

Per HalfCat bearing/tear-out formulas, shear area and bearing area scale with wall thickness `t`. Copying “6 bolts like Contrail” onto a **0.125″** custom tube is **not** structurally equivalent and under-predicts required `N` at 45 bar hydroproof.

Use Contrail only as a **same-OD interface reference**, not as a bolt-count recipe for the thin custom chamber.
